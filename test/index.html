About me

I like writing, in particular software to make mobile robots move, and fly. Oh, I really love see them flying. I am currently a PhD student in Aerial Robotics at University of Southern Denmmark under the supervision of professor Ulrik Pagh Schultz where I joined Unmanned Aerial Systems Center in fall 2018.

Personal
